If Ares (Mars) is with either the Moon or the Sun, he indicates the thieves are slaves or freedmen, and given to anger and violence. If the stars are under the rays of the Sun, they indicate the thieves are elders, and the Moon, old women; but those in the east (i.e., rising), outside, are younger, and those in the west, middle-aged. 

Also examine from the stars and their phases in this way: Hermes signifies children, Ares young men, Zeus those of middle age, Kronos elders.
